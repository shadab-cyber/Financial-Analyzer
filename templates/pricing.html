Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

Everything you need to know before choosing a plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All paid plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your account settings and you won't be charged again.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Pro comes with a 7-day free trial — no credit card required. You'll get full access to all six modules before committing.

What currencies are supported?

Pricing is in Indian Rupees (₹). Payments are processed via Razorpay and support all major Indian debit/credit cards and UPI.

What's included in Enterprise?

Enterprise plans are customised for your firm — covering seat count, API rate limits, white-label scope, and SLA. Contact us for a scoping call.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. Upgrades are prorated automatically.

Do you offer discounts for students or startups?

Yes — verified students and early-stage startups can apply for a 50% discount. Email us with proof of enrolment or incorporation.
