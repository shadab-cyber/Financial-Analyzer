Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before subscribing.

What's included in the 14-day free trial? +
The Pro trial gives you full, unrestricted access to all 7 tools for 14 days — no credit card required to start. You only need to pay if you choose to continue after the trial.
Can I use the tools on my phone? +
Yes. Financial Analyzer is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. An Android app wrapper is also available for a more native experience.
What data sources do the tools use? +
Stock prices and historical data come from NSE via Yahoo Finance (yfinance). Fundamental analysis uses Screener.in Excel exports which you upload directly. We don't store your financial data — it's processed in real time.
I'm a boutique IB / PE fund. What does Enterprise include? +
Enterprise is designed for small teams (2–20 analysts). It includes: multi-seat access with individual logins, a dedicated onboarding session, priority support with guaranteed response times, and the option for custom branding if you want to white-label the platform for client presentations. Pricing starts at ₹5,000/seat/month with volume discounts for 5+ seats. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
Is the financial analysis investment advice? +
No. Financial Analyzer provides analytical tools to help you research and model investments. Nothing on the platform constitutes investment advice, a buy/sell recommendation, or a guarantee of returns. Always verify data independently and consult a registered financial advisor before investing.
How accurate is the DCF and financial modelling? +
Our tools implement standard valuation methodologies used by professional analysts — WACC-based DCF, FCFF, Monte Carlo simulation, Altman Z-Score, Piotroski F-Score, and more. The accuracy of any output depends on the quality of the input data and the assumptions used. We show sensitivity tables and confidence scores so you always know the range of outcomes, not just a single number.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. You keep access until the end of the billing period. Annual plans can be cancelled but are non-refundable after the 14-day trial window.
